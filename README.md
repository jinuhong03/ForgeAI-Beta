# Cycle Log — PWA

A personal fitness tracker for workouts, nutrition, and progress.

## Deploy to Netlify (easiest)

1. Go to https://netlify.com and sign up for free
2. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag and drop the **cycle-log-pwa** folder onto the page
4. Your app is live at a URL like https://yourname.netlify.app

## Deploy to Vercel

1. Go to https://vercel.com and sign up for free
2. Click "Add New" → "Project" → "Deploy from CLI" or use the web drag-and-drop
3. Upload the cycle-log-pwa folder
4. Your app is live at https://yourproject.vercel.app

## Install on iPhone

1. Open your deployed URL in Safari (must be Safari — Chrome won't prompt)
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add" — it appears on your home screen like a native app

## Install on Android

1. Open your deployed URL in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"

## Notes

- All data is saved locally on your device via localStorage
- Data does NOT sync between devices
- Clearing your browser site data will erase logs
- The AI macro-estimate feature is not available in the standalone app —
  use the built-in food list or enter macros manually
